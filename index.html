Job Finder AI Agent
An intelligent, automated job search system for data science positions. Scrapes postings, runs NLP-based resume matching, classifies by H1B/MAANG/Fortune 500 signals, and delivers ranked results to Google Sheets with email alerts.
AI Agent System
  • Resume parsing — extracts skills, experience, and keywords from PDF
  • TF-IDF cosine similarity + keyword overlap scoring (0–100%)
  • H1B visa sponsorship signal detection
  • MAANG & Fortune 500 company classification
  • ATS portal detection (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever, etc.)
  • Google Sheets integration with conditional formatting
  • Automated schedule — Mon–Fri 9 AM, 11:30 AM, 4:30 PM
  • HTML email reports with ATS keyword suggestions
# Matching Algorithm
tfidf_score = cosine_similarity (resume, jd)
kw_score = skill_overlap (resume, jd)
 
# Weighted blend
match = ( 0.65 × tfidf_score
+ 0.35 × kw_score )
 
# Threshold: 72% to qualify
if match >= 0.72 : log_to_sheets ()
